In 2014, HIMANSHU GOENKA, then a 17 year old, visited a shelter home to spend a day with the children to bring some happiness in their lives. His experience fostered the desire to do this for the rest of his life. Over the last 10 years, MUSKURAHAT has grown from a group of young volunteers bringing smiles to children in need to an impact driven education NGO that is striving to provide access to quality education and a chance for a better future to all children in need. Our vision is to equip the next generation with knowledge and skills to not only survive but also thrive in a dynamic world, ensuring their long-term success and well-being.

We have been PROVIDING ACCESS TO QUALITY EDUCATION which fosters a child’s HOLISTIC DEVELOPMENT ,significantly increases their ability to make informed choices in life, enabling them to build a fulfilling life for themselves. We have done this through our Project KeyTAAB, Project Sakshaar, and Project Saarthi. Our programs align with Sustainable Development Goal 4 (SDG 4), which aims to ensure inclusive and equitable quality education for all, and the United Nations Convention on the Rights of the Child (UNCRC), specifically Article 28, which emphasises the right to education for every child.
